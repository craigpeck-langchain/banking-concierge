# Common Account Fees

## Monthly service fees
- Everyday Checking: $10 (waivable)
- Clear Access Banking: $5 (waivable for ages 13–24)
- Prime Checking: $25 (waivable with $20,000 combined balance)
- Premier Checking: $35 (waivable with $250,000 combined balance)
- Way2Save Savings: $5 (waivable)
- Platinum Savings: $12 (waivable with $3,500 daily balance)

## Overdraft and non-sufficient funds
- Overdraft fee: $35 per item, maximum 3 per business day
- No fee on items $5 or less
- No fee on overdrafts of $5 or less
- Extra Day Grace period: no overdraft fee if the negative balance is fully covered by the end of the next business day
- Non-sufficient funds (NSF) returned item fee: $0

## ATM fees
- Meridian National ATM: no fee for Meridian National accounts
- Non-Meridian National ATM, domestic: $2.50 per withdrawal, $2.50 per balance inquiry or transfer
- Non-Meridian National ATM, international: $5.00 per withdrawal, plus 3% currency conversion when applicable
- Prime and Premier Checking customers: no Meridian National fee at non-Meridian National ATMs (operator fees may still apply)

## Wire transfer fees
- Incoming domestic wire: $15 (waived for Prime/Premier Checking)
- Outgoing domestic wire (in-branch): $30
- Outgoing domestic wire (online): $25
- Incoming international wire: $16 (waived for Prime/Premier Checking)
- Outgoing international wire (in branch, U.S. dollars): $45
- Outgoing international wire (online, foreign currency): typically no Meridian National fee; exchange rate spread applies

## Other fees
- Stop payment: $31
- Cashier's check: $10 per check
- Statement copy: $5 per statement
- Money order (up to $1,000): $5
