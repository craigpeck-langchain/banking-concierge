# Account Transfers

## Internal transfers between Meridian National accounts
Free and immediate, 24/7, when initiated through online or mobile banking. There is no daily limit on internal transfers between your own Meridian National accounts.

## Transfers to another Meridian National customer
Free, immediate, 24/7. Requires the recipient's Meridian National account number and full name.

## Transfers to and from external bank accounts
Use Transfer & Pay → External Accounts. Standard delivery is 3 business days and free. Next-business-day delivery is $10 per transfer.

External account verification is required before the first transfer. Two test deposits will be sent within 2–3 business days; you confirm the amounts to verify ownership.

## Limits
- External transfers: $5,000 per day, $10,000 per 30-day period for standard delivery; $2,500 per day for next-day delivery (subject to risk-based limits)
- Internal transfers: no Meridian National dollar limit

## Scheduled and recurring transfers
You can schedule one-time transfers up to one year in advance, or set up recurring transfers (weekly, biweekly, monthly). Cancel any time at least one business day before the next scheduled date.

## Zelle
For payments to other people who bank elsewhere, see the Zelle help topic. Zelle is faster than an external transfer for person-to-person payments but has lower per-transaction limits.
