# Online Banking

## Signing up
Enroll at meridiannational.com using your Social Security number or ITIN, account or debit card number, email address, and date of birth. Enrollment is free and available to all Meridian National personal account holders.

## What you can do
- View and download account activity and statements
- Transfer money between Meridian National accounts and to/from external accounts
- Send and receive money with Zelle
- Pay bills with Bill Pay
- Manage debit and credit cards (lock/unlock, change PIN, set travel notices)
- Apply for new accounts, credit cards, and loans
- Update contact and security information
- Set up account alerts (low balance, large transaction, etc.)

## Security
- Two-step verification with a 6-digit code by text or voice
- Account activity alerts and instant security notifications
- Up to $0 liability for unauthorized online or mobile transactions reported promptly (see Zero Liability Protection in the Security topic)

## Browser requirements
Use a current major browser (latest two versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari). Cookies and JavaScript must be enabled. TLS 1.2 or above.

## Sessions
Sessions time out after 10 minutes of inactivity for your protection. Always sign off and close the browser when you finish.
