# Debit Cards

## Daily limits
- ATM cash withdrawal: $300 default, up to $1,500 on request
- Point-of-sale purchases and debit transactions: $5,000 default
- Limits can be temporarily increased; call 1-800-555-0100 or visit a branch

## Activate a new card
Activate in the Meridian National Mobile app under Menu → Cards, online at meridiannational.com/activate, or by calling 1-877-555-0177. Cards arrive in 5–7 business days.

## Lock or unlock your card
Available 24/7 in the mobile app or online. Locking prevents new purchases, ATM withdrawals, and most recurring billing. Existing pre-authorized payments may still post.

## Change your PIN
- In the mobile app: Menu → Cards → Manage debit card → Change PIN
- Online: Manage Cards → Change PIN
- At any Meridian National ATM with your current PIN
- By phone: 1-800-555-0100

## Replacement card
Request a replacement in the app or online (Manage Cards → Report lost/stolen). Standard delivery is 5–7 business days at no cost; expedited delivery is $15.

## Travel notices
Travel notices are generally no longer required. Meridian National uses behavioral analytics to monitor transactions while you travel. Make sure your contact information is up to date so you can respond to fraud alerts.
