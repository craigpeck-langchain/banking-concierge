# Branches and ATMs

## Finding a branch or ATM
- Use the Locator at meridiannational.com/locator
- Use the Locator in the Meridian National Mobile app
- Filter by services: drive-up, ATM-only, notary, safe deposit boxes, Saturday hours

## ATM access
- About 11,000 Meridian National ATMs across the United States
- Cardless ATM access: sign in to the mobile app, select an ATM transaction, and tap your phone at the ATM (NFC) or use a one-time access code

## Branch hours
Standard branch hours are typically Monday–Friday 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Saturday 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM, but vary by location. Some branches are open Sunday. Always confirm in the Locator before visiting.

## Services at branches
- Cash deposits and withdrawals
- Cashier's checks and money orders
- Wire transfers
- Notary public (no fee for customers; appointment recommended)
- Safe deposit boxes (availability and pricing vary by branch)
- Open new accounts
- Mortgage and lending consultations

## Closed-branch and successor-branch service
If your home branch closed, your accounts move to the nearest open branch automatically and your account number does not change. Direct deposits, autopays, and recurring transfers are unaffected.
