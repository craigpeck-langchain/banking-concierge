# Savings Accounts

Meridian National offers two personal savings accounts.

## Way2Save Savings
A simple starter savings account. Monthly service fee is $5, waived with one of the following each fee period:
- $300 minimum daily balance
- One automatic transfer of $25 or more from a linked Meridian National checking account
- One or more recurring automatic transfers of $1 or more from a linked Meridian National checking account per business day
- A primary account owner under age 25

Standard interest rate (APY) is 0.01%. Relationship interest rates apply when linked to a Prime or Premier Checking account.

Minimum opening deposit: $25.

## Platinum Savings
An interest-bearing savings account with tiered rates. Monthly service fee is $12, waived with a $3,500 minimum daily balance.

Standard interest rates (APY) are tiered by balance and refreshed monthly. As of the most recent rate sheet date on file, the standard tier APY ranges from 0.05% to 0.40%. Special introductory rate offers may be available for new money; consult the current rate sheet for the latest figures.

Minimum opening deposit: $25.
