# Direct Deposit

## Setting up direct deposit
Give your employer (or other payer) Meridian National's routing number for your state and your account number. Or share a pre-filled direct deposit form from online banking.

- Get the form: sign on → Account Services → Direct Deposit Form
- The form includes your account number and the correct routing number for your account
- The routing number depends on the state where the account was opened. Common routing numbers: California 999000001, Texas 999000002, New York 999000003. Always verify in your direct deposit form before sharing.

## Timing
- Funds are generally available the morning of the scheduled payday
- Some employers send payroll up to two business days early; with Early Pay Day, Meridian National posts qualifying direct deposits as soon as we receive them—often up to two days early
- Eligible deposits: ACH credits coded as payroll, government benefit, or pension

## Verifying a direct deposit
Direct deposits show up under recent activity with the source name (employer or agency) and a "DIRECT DEPOSIT" descriptor. You can also enable an Account Alert: "Direct deposit posted."

## Changing or stopping direct deposit
Update with your employer's payroll system; Meridian National cannot route or stop a deposit on your behalf.
