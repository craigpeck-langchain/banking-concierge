# International Banking

## Using your debit card abroad
- Meridian National debit cards work at most international merchants and ATMs that display the Visa or Plus logos
- Non-Meridian National ATM, international: $5.00 per withdrawal, plus a 3% currency conversion fee where applicable
- Travel notices are generally not required; make sure your contact information is current to respond to fraud alerts
- Notify Meridian National of long trips with frequent transactions by sending a secure message in online banking

## Using your credit card abroad
- Meridian National personal credit cards do not charge a foreign transaction fee on the Autograph and Autograph Journey
- The Active Cash Card charges a 3% foreign transaction fee
- Always pay in the local currency rather than U.S. dollars at the point of sale to avoid Dynamic Currency Conversion markups

## International wires
See the Wires help topic. Outgoing international wires in foreign currency are typically no Meridian National fee online; the exchange-rate spread applies. Incoming international wires are $16, waived for Prime/Premier Checking.

## ExpressSend (remittances)
ExpressSend is Meridian National's lower-cost remittance service to participating banks in select countries (Mexico, Philippines, India, several Central/South American countries). Fees start at $5 per transfer; check the current ExpressSend page for country list and exchange rates.

## Foreign currency
Order foreign currency online for delivery in about 2–3 business days to your home or a branch. Cash pickup also available at select branches. Buy-back of unused currency available at most branches.
