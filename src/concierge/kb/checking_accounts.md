# Checking Accounts

Meridian National offers three personal checking account tiers.

## Everyday Checking
The standard everyday checking account. Monthly service fee is $10, waived when you meet one of the following requirements each fee period:
- $500 or more in qualifying electronic deposits
- $1,500 minimum daily balance
- A linked Meridian National Campus ATM Card or Campus Debit Card for primary account owners aged 17–24
- Account opened with a primary account owner under age 25

Minimum opening deposit: $25.

## Clear Access Banking
A checkless account designed for early banking customers. Monthly service fee is $5, waived for primary account owners aged 13–24. No overdraft fees because there are no checks.

Minimum opening deposit: $25.

## Prime Checking
An interest-bearing checking account with relationship benefits. Monthly service fee is $25, waived with a $20,000 minimum combined balance across qualifying linked deposit, savings, and investment accounts.

Benefits include:
- No fees on incoming domestic and international wires
- No fees at non-Meridian National ATMs (the other bank's fees may still apply)
- Discount on a home equity line of credit interest rate
- Bonus interest rate on linked eligible savings accounts

Minimum opening deposit: $25.

## Premier Checking
The top relationship tier. Monthly service fee is $35, waived with a $250,000 minimum combined qualifying balance. Includes all Prime Checking benefits plus a higher interest rate, dedicated relationship banker, and a discount on a Meridian National Premier Mortgage origination fee.

Minimum opening deposit: $25.
