# Personal Credit Cards

## Card lineup (summary)
- Active Cash Card: 2% cash rewards on purchases, $0 annual fee, intro APR offers may apply
- Autograph Card: 3X points on restaurants, travel, gas, transit, popular streaming, and phone plans; 1X elsewhere; $0 annual fee
- Autograph Journey Card: 5X on Meridian National hotels via the rewards portal, 4X on airlines, 3X on other travel and dining, 1X on other purchases; $95 annual fee
- Reflect Card: 0% intro APR on purchases and qualifying balance transfers for 21 months from account opening; $0 annual fee
- Choice Privileges Visa Signature: hotel rewards card co-branded with Choice Hotels
- Bilt Mastercard: rewards on rent, dining, and travel; $0 annual fee

Specific APRs, rewards rates, and intro offers change. Always check the Application and Solicitation Disclosure for current terms.

## Common credit card features
- Zero liability protection for unauthorized charges reported promptly
- Cell phone protection on the Autograph and Autograph Journey when you pay your phone bill with the card
- 24/7 fraud monitoring
- Add cards to Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Wallet

## Manage your credit card
- View statements and pay your bill in online or mobile banking
- Lock/unlock your credit card from the mobile app
- Set up alerts for due dates, posted transactions, and credit limit usage
- Request a credit limit increase: online → Account Services → Request a Credit Limit Increase

## Late payment and APR
- Late fee: up to $40
- Penalty APR: not applied on most cards; check your Cardmember Agreement
- Grace period for purchases: at least 25 days from the close of each billing cycle if the previous balance is paid in full by the due date
