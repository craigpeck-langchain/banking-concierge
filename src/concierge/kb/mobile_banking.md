# Mobile Banking

## Meridian National Mobile app
Available for iOS (iOS 16 and above) and Android (Android 10 and above). Free to download and use, though your mobile carrier's data rates may apply.

## What you can do
- View account balances and recent activity
- Pay bills and people (Zelle and Bill Pay)
- Transfer between Meridian National accounts and to external accounts
- Deposit checks with Mobile Deposit
- View and download statements (up to 7 years)
- Lock and unlock your debit card
- Set up account alerts
- Find branches and ATMs
- Chat with a banker

## Mobile Deposit limits
- Standard limit: $20,000 per 30 calendar days, up to 25 checks
- Higher limits may be available based on account history; check the limits screen in the app
- Funds availability: generally next business day; up to $300 may be available immediately

## Biometric sign-on
Use Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint on supported devices. Enable in the app under Menu → Security Center → Sign-on & Security.

## Two-step verification
Required for new device sign-ins. A 6-digit code is sent by text or voice call to a phone number on file. Authenticator app support is available in the Security Center.

## Trouble signing in
Use "Forgot username or password?" on the sign-on screen. If your account is locked, call 1-800-555-0100 (24/7).
