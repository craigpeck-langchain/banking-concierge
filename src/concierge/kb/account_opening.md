# Opening a New Account

## Ways to open an account
- Online at meridiannational.com (most personal accounts)
- In the Meridian National Mobile app
- In a branch

## What you'll need
- Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, state ID, or passport)
- Social Security number or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN)
- Date of birth
- Current physical U.S. address
- Email address and a U.S. mobile number
- Funding source: existing Meridian National account, debit/credit card from another bank, or external bank account for ACH

## Minimum opening deposit
- Checking accounts: $25 minimum opening deposit
- Way2Save and Platinum Savings: $25 minimum opening deposit
- Some special accounts and CDs have higher minimums

## Eligibility
- Most accounts: age 18 or older (younger ages allowed for Clear Access Banking and Way2Save as a joint owner with a parent or guardian)
- U.S. resident with a valid taxpayer identification number
- No previous account closures with Meridian National for cause that remain unresolved

## After opening
- You'll receive a welcome packet and your debit card within 5–7 business days
- Activate your card in the mobile app, online, or by calling 1-877-555-0177
- Enroll in online and mobile banking using your new account number and SSN/ITIN

## Joint accounts and beneficiaries
Add a joint owner during the application or later in a branch. Add Payable-on-Death (POD) beneficiaries free of charge through the secure message center or in a branch.
