# Wire Transfers

## Outgoing domestic wires
- Initiated online: $25
- Initiated in-branch or by phone: $30
- Same-day if submitted before the cutoff time (typically 2:00 PM Pacific on a business day)

## Incoming domestic wires
- $15 fee, waived for Prime Checking and Premier Checking customers
- Posted same-day when received before end-of-day cutoff

## Outgoing international wires
- In branch in U.S. dollars: $45 fee
- In branch in foreign currency: no Meridian National fee; exchange-rate spread applies
- Online in foreign currency: no Meridian National fee; exchange-rate spread applies
- Delivery time: typically 1–2 business days

## Incoming international wires
- $16 fee, waived for Prime Checking and Premier Checking customers
- Meridian National's SWIFT/BIC code is **MERIUS33**

## Daily wire limits
- Online outgoing wires: $250,000 per business day default; higher limits available on request through your banker
- In-branch wires: no fixed Meridian National dollar cap; subject to verification

## What you need to send a wire
- Recipient name and physical address
- Recipient account number (or IBAN for international)
- Recipient bank name and routing number (or SWIFT/BIC for international)
- For international wires: intermediary bank instructions if required by the destination bank

## Tracing a wire
Call Meridian National Wire Services at 1-800-CALL-MNB once 2 business days have passed without confirmation. Provide the IMAD/OMAD reference number from your transfer confirmation.
