# Disputing a Charge

## Debit card disputes
To dispute a debit card purchase or ATM transaction, sign on to online or mobile banking and select the transaction. Choose "Dispute this transaction" and follow the prompts. You can also call 1-800-555-0100.

Common dispute reasons:
- Unauthorized transaction (fraud)
- Duplicate charge
- Goods or services not received
- Goods or services not as described
- Incorrect amount

Provisional credit is typically issued within 10 business days while the investigation is underway. Investigations generally complete within 45 days. For unauthorized debit transactions reported within 60 days of the statement, Zero Liability Protection applies.

## Credit card disputes
Sign on to online or mobile banking, find the transaction, and select "Dispute". You can also call the number on the back of your card.

Federal billing rights protect credit card holders: you generally have 60 days from the statement date to dispute a billing error.

## Information you'll need
- Transaction date and amount
- Merchant name
- Description of the issue
- For "not as described" or "not received": evidence of your attempt to resolve with the merchant first
- Any supporting receipts, screenshots, or correspondence

## After you file
You'll receive a confirmation email with a case number. Track status in the mobile app under Menu → Disputes, or online under Account Services → Dispute Tracker. Most disputes resolve within 45 days; complex cases (international or extended-investigation merchants) may take up to 90 days.
