# Autopay and Bill Pay

## Bill Pay
Schedule one-time and recurring payments to companies and individuals from your Meridian National checking account. Free with most personal checking accounts.

- Sign on → Transfer & Pay → Bill Pay
- Add a payee with name, account number, and address (or pick from popular billers)
- Schedule a one-time payment or recurring schedule (weekly, monthly, etc.)
- Most electronic payments arrive in 1–2 business days; check delivery times listed when you schedule

## Autopay on Meridian National credit cards
Set up automatic monthly payments from any Meridian National or external bank checking or savings account.
- Pay the Statement Balance, Minimum Payment, or a Fixed Amount each month
- Payments post on the due date
- Enroll: Sign on → Credit Card → Automatic Payments

## Autopay on Meridian National loans (auto, personal, mortgage)
Set up under Account Services for the loan. A 0.25% interest rate discount may be available when you set up automatic payments at origination on certain consumer loans; verify in your loan disclosure.

## Stopping a payment
- Bill Pay: cancel the scheduled payment in the Bill Pay screen before the send date. Once sent, you cannot recall an electronic Bill Pay payment.
- Check-based Bill Pay: a stop payment may be possible if the check has not been cashed; $31 fee may apply.
- Recurring debits initiated by a merchant: contact the merchant first; you can also file a stop payment with Meridian National (fee may apply) and dispute any unauthorized debit.
