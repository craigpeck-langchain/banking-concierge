# Statements

## Online statements
Available free for up to 7 years in online and mobile banking. Sign on → Statements & Documents → Statements. Select an account and a statement period to view or download as PDF.

## Paper statements
Available by mail for a $2.00 monthly fee per account on most accounts. The fee is waived for accounts whose primary owner is age 65 or older. Switch to paperless in Statements & Documents → Statements & Documents Preferences.

## Statement cycles
- Checking and savings: monthly, on a date based on when the account was opened
- Credit cards: monthly, on a fixed cycle date you can see in your last statement
- Combined statements: if your accounts share an address and ownership, they may print on one combined statement

## Statement copies older than 7 years
Request through online banking (Statements & Documents → Request a Statement Copy) or by calling 1-800-555-0100. Older statement copies are $5.00 per statement and arrive in 5–7 business days.

## Year-end tax documents
1099-INT and 1099-DIV (when applicable) are typically available by January 31 in Statements & Documents → Tax Documents. Mortgage 1098 is typically available by January 31 as well.
