# Overdraft Coverage

## Overdraft fee
$35 per item when an item posts to your account and your available balance is negative. Maximum of 3 overdraft fees per business day. No fee on items of $5 or less and no fee when the overdraft amount is $5 or less.

## Extra Day Grace period
If your account is overdrawn at the end of the business day, you have until the end of the next business day to bring your available balance to $0 or positive. If you do, the overdraft fees from the previous day are reversed.

## Overdraft Protection
Link an eligible Meridian National savings account or credit account to your checking account. If an item would overdraw your checking account, funds are transferred automatically from your linked account in the exact amount needed (in $1 increments). The Overdraft Protection transfer is free.

## Debit Card Overdraft Service (opt-in)
Allows everyday debit card and ATM transactions to be authorized when your available balance is insufficient. Standard $35 overdraft fee applies per item if the transaction posts and your balance is negative. You may opt in or out at any time.

## Items that cannot be overdrafted
- Checks and ACH for $5 or less
- Items where your available balance is below $5 negative

## Returned item (NSF) fee
$0. Meridian National does not charge a fee when an item is returned unpaid for non-sufficient funds.
